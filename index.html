About Us

Crafting Unforgettable Safari Experiences Since 2010

MMexy Tanzania Adventures is a premier tourism company specializing in authentic Tanzanian experiences. With over a decade of excellence, we guide discerning travelers to discover the true beauty of Tanzania.

We provide exceptional services to our guests, offering them a unique and immersive experience of Tanzania's rich cultural heritage and natural wonders.

Our Tour Packages

❖

Every traveler deserves an experience tailored to their needs. Explore our distinct tiers designed for different levels of comfort and exclusivity.

🌿 Authentic

Local Package

Ideal for adventurous souls who want to witness the magic of Tanzania while staying mindful of their budget.

  • Shared safari vehicles (Window seat guaranteed)
  • Quality public campsites or cozy local guesthouses
  • Three fresh, local meals daily by bush chefs
Best for: Solo travelers & budget-conscious groups.
⭐ Comfort

VIP Package

A step above the ordinary, offering enhanced comfort and a more personalized pace away from the crowds.

  • Private 4x4 Land Cruiser exclusively for your party
  • Hand-picked 3 to 4-star mid-range lodges/tented camps
  • Dedicated professional guide & priority access
Best for: Families, Couples, and Honeymooners.
👑 Exclusive

VVIP Package

The pinnacle of Tanzanian luxury. Every detail is curated to ensure a seamless and prestigious journey.

  • Private charter flights between parks & luxury vehicles
  • Ultra-luxury 5-star lodges & exclusive boutique retreats
  • 24/7 personal concierge, fine dining, & premium drinks
Best for: Luxury seekers and high-profile guests.
