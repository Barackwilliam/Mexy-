5-Day Zanzibar Beach Escape Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Zanzibar
Beach Resort
Arrive at Zanzibar International Airport, transfer to your beachfront resort. Settle in and enjoy your first sunset over the Indian Ocean. Welcome dinner and orientation.
Day 2: Beach Relaxation & Water Sports
Full Day Beach Activities
Morning at leisure to enjoy the beach. Afternoon snorkeling session to explore the coral reefs. Optional water sports activities including kayaking and paddle boarding.
Day 3: Spice Tour & Stone Town
Cultural Exploration
Morning spice plantation tour to learn about Zanzibar's famous spices. Afternoon guided tour of historic Stone Town, visiting the Old Fort, House of Wonders, and local markets.
Day 4: Prison Island & Dolphin Tour
Island Excursion
Boat trip to Prison Island to see giant tortoises and enjoy snorkeling. Afternoon dolphin watching tour in Kizimkazi. Sunset dhow cruise with traditional music.
Day 5: Departure
Farewell
Morning at leisure for last-minute beach time and souvenir shopping. Transfer to Zanzibar International Airport for departure with unforgettable memories.
