Kilimanjaro Expedition

5-9 Days of Unforgettable Mountain Adventure

About This Adventure

Embark on the ultimate African adventure as you conquer Mount Kilimanjaro, the highest peak in Africa at 5,895 meters (19,341 feet). Our expertly guided expeditions take you through lush rainforests, alpine meadows, and arctic summit zones.

Key Highlights

  • Summit Africa's highest peak - Uhuru Peak
  • Experience 5 distinct climate zones
  • Professional mountain guides and porters
  • All safety equipment provided
  • Comfortable mountain huts and camps

Expedition Details

Our carefully crafted itineraries ensure optimal acclimatization and the highest success rates. Choose from multiple routes including Marangu, Machame, Lemosho, and Rongai, each offering unique experiences and challenges.

What Makes Our Expeditions Special

  • Highest success rate in the industry
  • Small group sizes for personalized attention
  • Premium equipment and safety protocols
  • Sustainable and responsible climbing practices
  • Celebration dinner and certificate upon summit

8-Day Lemosho Route Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Tanzania
Arusha Hotel

Arrive at Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO), meet our representative, and transfer to your hotel in Arusha. Evening briefing with your lead guide, equipment check, and orientation session.

Day 2: Lemosho Gate to Mti Mkubwa Camp
2,650m | 5-6 hours

Morning drive to Lemosho Gate. Trek through lush montane rainforest. Spot black and white colobus monkeys, blue monkeys, and various bird species.

Day 3–6: Acclimatization & Ascent
Shira → Barranco → Karanga → Barafu

Trek across the Shira Plateau, ascend Lava Tower for acclimatization, conquer Barranco Wall, and arrive at Barafu base camp for the summit push.

Day 7: Summit Day – Uhuru Peak (5,895m)
12–15 hours

Midnight start. Reach Stella Point at dawn for sunrise over Africa, then continue to Uhuru Peak — the highest point on the continent. Descend to Mweka Camp.

Day 8: Descent to Mweka Gate
3-4 hours

Final descent through rainforest. Receive official summit certificates. Celebration with your climbing team, then transfer to Arusha.

Kilimanjaro Day Hike

Experience Africa's highest mountain in a single unforgettable day — no summit required. Hike through lush rainforest to Mandara Hut on the scenic Marangu Route.

About This Tour

This day hike is ideal for travelers who want to experience Mount Kilimanjaro without climbing to the summit.

Starting at Marangu Gate (1,800 m), the trail winds through lush tropical rainforest all the way up to Mandara Hut (2,743 m) — offering a true feel of Africa's highest free-standing mountain in just one day.
