5-9 Days of Unforgettable Mountain Adventure

About This Adventure

Embark on the ultimate African adventure as you conquer Mount Kilimanjaro, the highest peak in Africa at 5,895 meters (19,341 feet). Our expertly guided expeditions take you through lush rainforests, alpine meadows, and arctic summit zones.

Key Highlights

  • Summit Africa's highest peak - Uhuru Peak
  • Experience 5 distinct climate zones
  • Professional mountain guides and porters
  • All safety equipment provided
  • Comfortable mountain huts and camps

8-Day Lemosho Route Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Tanzania
Arusha Hotel
Arrive at Kilimanjaro International Airport, transfer to your hotel in Arusha. Briefing with your guide and equipment check.
Day 2: Lemosho Gate to Mti Mkubwa
2,650m - 5-6 hours
Drive to Lemosho Gate, begin trek through lush rainforest to Mti Mkubwa camp. Spot colobus monkeys and rich forest flora.
Day 3: Mti Mkubwa to Shira 1 Camp
3,610m - 6-7 hours
Ascend through moorland zone with spectacular views of Kibo Peak. Acclimatization hike in the afternoon.
Day 4: Shira 1 Camp to Barranco Camp
3,960m - 6-7 hours
Trek across Shira Plateau, then ascend to Lava Tower (4,630m) for acclimatization before descending to Barranco Valley.
Day 5: Barranco Camp to Karanga Camp
4,035m - 4-5 hours
Conquer the Barranco Wall, then traverse valleys and ridges to Karanga Camp with stunning views of the southern ice fields.
Day 6: Karanga Camp to Barafu Camp
4,640m - 4-5 hours
Ascend to Barafu Camp, the base camp for your summit attempt. Early dinner and rest before midnight summit start.
Day 7: Summit Day - Barafu to Uhuru to Mweka
5,895m - 12-15 hours
Midnight start for summit attempt. Reach Uhuru Peak at sunrise, celebrate your achievement, then descend to Mweka Camp.
Day 8: Mweka Camp to Mweka Gate
1,640m - 3-4 hours
Final descent through rainforest to Mweka Gate. Receive summit certificates, transfer to hotel for celebration and rest.
